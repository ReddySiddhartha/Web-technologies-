Dear Comrade - Movie Review

Dear Comrade is a Telugu romantic drama film starring Vijay Deverakonda and Rashmika Mandanna. The movie beautifully blends romance with a strong social message about fighting for justice. It focuses on Bobby, a student leader, and Lilly, a cricketer, as they navigate love, ambition, and struggles against injustice.

Highlights of the Movie

  • Strong performances by the lead actors
  • Emotional and inspiring storyline
  • Powerful music by Justin Prabhakaran

Top 3 Reasons to Watch

  1. Heart-touching love story
  2. Social message about standing up for what is right
  3. Excellent background score and songs
